Processes bulk claims via Excel upload/download,
 running the full validation pipeline across 
 thousands of claims in a single batch with 
 structured results output.

Uses an LLM to generate a plain-language 
explanation for each Valid/Invalid/Needs Review 
verdict, citing the specific plan document rule
 that supports the outcome.

 Automatically classifies and explains claim
  rejections by standard reject codes 
  (e.g., Benefit Exclusion, Prior Authorization
   Required, Plan Limitations Exceeded).

Provides a live dashboard showing scenario 
breakdowns (Paid-Paid, Paid-Reject, Reject-Reject,
 Reject-Paid), validation status, and drill-down
  by claim number.


Parses BPS and CPM plan documents into a knowledge
 graph and vector index, enabling precise retrieval
  of copay, tier, PA, and exclusion rules for 
  validation.


Automatically compares claims run in production
 against the same claims re-run in the certification
  environment, flagging status, copay, tier, and
   reject-code mismatches.
